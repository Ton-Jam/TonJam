## Getting Started

When you first open TonJam, you'll be greeted by the **Discover** page, featuring trending tracks, top artists, and curated playlists. You can start listening to music immediately, but to unlock the full potential of the platform—including buying NFTs, earning rewards, and interacting with artists—you'll need to connect your TON wallet.

## Wallet Connection

TonJam uses the TON blockchain for secure, decentralized transactions. Connecting your wallet is your key to the platform.

### How to Connect Your Wallet:
1. Look for the **"Connect Wallet"** button in the top right corner of the navigation bar.
2. Click the button to open the TON Connect modal.
3. Choose your preferred TON wallet (e.g., Tonkeeper, Tonhub, or Telegram Wallet).
4. Scan the QR code with your mobile wallet app or approve the connection if using a browser extension.
5. Once connected, your wallet address will be displayed, and you'll have access to premium features, the marketplace, and your JAM token balance.

*Note: You need a small amount of TON in your wallet to cover network gas fees for transactions like buying NFTs or staking JAM.*

## Music Discovery & Streaming

TonJam offers a seamless music streaming experience with a focus on electronic, synthwave, and decentralized beats.

*   **Discover:** Explore new releases, trending tracks, and featured artists on the home page.
*   **Player:** The persistent audio player at the bottom of the screen lets you control playback, adjust volume, and view the current track. Click the expand icon to open the full-screen player for an immersive visualizer experience.
*   **Playlists:** Browse curated playlists or create your own.
*   **Jam Rooms:** Join live listening rooms to experience music simultaneously with other users.

## The Marketplace

The TonJam Marketplace is where you can buy, sell, and trade exclusive music NFTs. These NFTs can represent digital collectibles, stems, or even royalty shares.

### Exploring the Marketplace:
1. Navigate to the **Marketplace** tab from the main menu.
2. Browse available NFTs, filter by genre, or search for specific artists.
3. Each NFT listing displays its price (in TON), edition type (e.g., Unique, Rare), and whether stems are available.

### Buying an NFT:
1. Click on an NFT to view its details.
2. If it's a fixed-price listing, click **"Buy Now"**.
3. If it's an auction, enter your bid amount and click **"Place Bid"**.
4. Your connected TON wallet will prompt you to confirm the transaction.
5. Once confirmed on the blockchain, the NFT will be added to your collection.

## Profile Management

Your profile is your identity on TonJam. It showcases your collection, your favorite tracks, and your activity.

### Customizing Your Profile:
1. Navigate to your **Profile** page.
2. Click the **"Edit Profile"** button.
3. Update your display name, handle, bio, and social links.
4. You can also change your avatar and banner images.
5. Save your changes to update your public persona.

### Viewing Your Assets:
Your profile page is divided into several tabs:
*   **Collection:** View all the music NFTs you currently own.
*   **Created:** (For Artists) See the NFTs you have minted.
*   **Activity:** Track your recent transactions, including streams, purchases, and staking rewards.

## JAM Token & Staking

**JAM** is the native utility token of the TonJam platform. It is used to reward users, boost tracks, and unlock premium features.

### Earning and Using JAM:
*   **Streaming:** Artists earn TON and JAM based on their streams.
*   **Purchasing:** You can buy JAM directly using TON from the Wallet/Premium section.
*   **Jamming Tracks:** Spend JAM to "boost" a track, increasing its visibility on the platform and directly supporting the artist.

### Staking JAM:
Staking your JAM tokens allows you to earn passive rewards over time.
1. Go to the **Wallet** or **Premium** section.
2. Locate the Staking dashboard.
3. Enter the amount of JAM you wish to stake and confirm the transaction.
4. Your staked JAM will generate rewards (e.g., 15% APR).
5. You can claim your accumulated rewards or unstake your tokens at any time.
